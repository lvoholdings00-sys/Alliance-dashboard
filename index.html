Alliance Tier Structure
Tier I
Principal

Founding members of LVO Holdings. Equity holders in the parent entity with full governance rights. Restricted to family members only.

Tier II
Associate

Trusted professionals in the LVO orbit. Attorneys, CPAs, contractors, lenders. Preferred access, consistent business, no equity.

Tier III
Partner

Co-investors brought in deal by deal. Earn deal-level equity. Advancement based on performance, not position.

Tier IV
Member

Starting position within the LVO community. Prove your worth and advance through results.
